Cake Bakery

A bakery is a fun place where yummy treats like cakes, cookies, and bread are made. Bakers wake up early to mix flour, sugar, and other things to make these treats. They use big ovens to bake them. When you walk by a bakery, you can smell the sweet scent of fresh bread or cookies. You can see bakers wearing white aprons and hats. They work hard to make sure the food is tasty and looks good. Some bakeries also make sandwiches and pizza. Going to a bakery is exciting because you can choose your favorite treat to eat.
